Victor Kong is the founder and CEO of Treantly, a remote hiring platform connecting North American businesses with vetted professionals in the Philippines. He started the company after seeing firsthand how broken offshore hiring had become: businesses getting burned by unreliable hires, and talented professionals left without the support they deserved.

"Offshore hiring should work better, for everyone involved. Treantly exists to rebuild that trust."

Victor's approach is straightforward: understand the real business problem first, then find the right person. Treantly acts as a recruitment partner, employer of record, and long-term support system - not just a resume service. Every professional placed comes with healthcare, fair employment, and a team behind them.

Beyond Treantly, Victor hosts the 1 on 1 podcast, where he sits down with founders, operators, and business builders for candid conversations on growth, remote work, and what it actually takes to build something real. No fluff. Just honest dialogue.

His belief is simple: great work comes from people who are supported, respected, and treated fairly. That philosophy drives everything at Treantly - from how they recruit to how they retain.
